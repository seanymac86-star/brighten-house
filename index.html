The Property

A home that gets it.

Brighten sits on Brighten Drive in Lewes, Delaware — technically not Rehoboth, which is exactly the point. Quieter streets, shorter lines, easier parking, same water.

The house is a three-bedroom townhouse-style condo built in 2005 with the kind of layout that actually works for a group: a primary suite downstairs for whoever gets there first, two more bedrooms upstairs, a generous loft for when people need their own corner, and a gas fireplace for the nights that call for it.

It's not a rental property. It belongs to friends. There are house rules, but they are delivered with love.

  • Primary suite on main floor — en suite bath, walk-in closet
  • Two additional bedrooms upstairs
  • Loft family room — movies, games, the retreat zone
  • Gas fireplace in the main living area
  • Vaulted ceilings, recessed lighting, hardwood floors
  • Full kitchen — light wood cabinetry, ceramic tile
  • 4 bathrooms. Yes, four. No waiting.
